Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing

What does "Pay-as-you-go" mean? +
Pay-as-you-go means you only pay for what you use beyond the base plan. For Basic plan, you pay $15 per million tokens (MTok) for AI processing. Most simple apps use 10-50K tokens, so you'll typically pay $0.15-$0.75 per app generation.
How do tokens work? +
Tokens are units of AI processing. Simple apps (like a todo list) use ~10-20K tokens. Complex apps (like a full SaaS platform) might use 50-100K tokens. You can see exact usage before generating each app.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes! You can cancel your subscription at any time. Your apps will continue to work, but you'll lose access to premium features and fall back to the free plan limits.
What's included in the free plan? +
The free plan includes 5 app generations per day, access to core templates, basic customization, and deployment to a Flaredream subdomain. Perfect for learning and small projects.
Do I own the code that's generated? +
Yes! You have full ownership of all generated code. You can modify, redistribute, or commercialize it however you like. We never claim ownership of your apps.
Is there a yearly discount? +
Yes! When you switch to yearly billing, you get 2 months free (save 17%). The Basic plan becomes $200/year instead of $240/year.
